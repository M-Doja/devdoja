A- Creativity is the beginning

For years I've created, written, and performed music for people all over the world. I started in my early teens discovering my creative side, first with music, then with paint and soon after, other outlets as well. Fastforward 3 years ago, this is when I became interested in computer code after watching a friend make a website from scratch. Seeing what could be done it almost seemed like magic, but no, it was just javascript.
