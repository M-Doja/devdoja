Creativity is the beginning

For years I've created, written, and performed music for people all over the world. I started in my early teens discovering my creative side, first with music, then with paint and soon after, other outlets as well. Fastforward 3 years ago, this is when I started working on computer programming after watching a friend make a website from scratch. Seeing what could be done it almost seemed like magic, but nope, it was just javascript.

Since that time I have completed a number of online courses from reputable websites like Team TreeHouse, Codecadmey, and FreeCoderCamp ranging from WordPress basics to Full Stack Javascript development in order to hone my skills. Most recently I worked with a team on a project at Coder Camps, a bootcamp style school for teaching how to develop using the MEAN stack, to develop a social media style web application for new developers called Coder-Net.
